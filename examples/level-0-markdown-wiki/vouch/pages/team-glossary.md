Shared vocabulary for the acme-example platform team. A living wiki page;
edits land via the normal review gate but the content is just definitions.

## Terms

- **monorepo** -- the single `acme-example/platform` repository that holds
  the API, web app, and shared libraries.
- **staging** -- the pre-production environment that mirrors prod; safe to
  break.
- **onboarding buddy** -- the engineer assigned to a new hire for their
  first two weeks.
- **starter ticket** -- a deliberately small, well-scoped issue used to
  walk a new engineer through the full ship cycle.
